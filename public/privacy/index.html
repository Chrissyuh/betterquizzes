Privacy Policy

BetterQuizzes helps people take interactive quizzes generated through ChatGPT. This page explains the data the app handles.

What the app handles

  • Quiz content, including questions, answer choices, and answer keys provided by ChatGPT.
  • Your quiz responses, confidence ratings, draft progress, and submission status.
  • Grade writeback data when ChatGPT records a score or feedback for a submitted quiz.
  • Basic technical data needed to load the widget, recover drafts, and display results.

How data is used

Data is used to render the quiz, save progress, submit answers, request grading, and show grade feedback. BetterQuizzes does not sell personal data.

Storage

Drafts and submissions may be stored temporarily in the browser or server memory so the widget can recover from refreshes and show grades. Hosted server memory may reset during redeploys or restarts.

Third parties

BetterQuizzes is designed for use inside ChatGPT. Your use of ChatGPT is also governed by OpenAI’s terms and privacy policy.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the BetterQuizzes developer through the project’s support channel or GitHub repository.
